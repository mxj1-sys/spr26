About

In Flight explores how birds are seen, misunderstood, and valued.

Birds are everywhere, but people do not always see them the same way. Some are admired for their color, song, or symbolism, while others are dismissed as pests or background noise.

01

Perception

This site looks at how human attitudes shape the way different birds are noticed, loved, feared, or ignored.

02

Ecology

Each bird plays a role in its environment, from spreading seeds to controlling insects to adapting within human-made spaces.

03

Observation

Through pictorials, reviews, maps, and features, this project encourages viewers to slow down and look more closely.

Who we are

Rose, Amethyst, and Jade

We are avid bird enthusiasts with an interest in how science, perception, and everyday environments intersect.

This project grew out of a curiosity about the birds we see daily but rarely think about and how quickly we assign meaning to them without understanding their roles.

Outside of this work, we run a non-for-profit raising funding for endangered bird species and spend time observing both natural and urban ecosystems.
